Project Gemini

Travel at it's Finest!

Gemini is for the person who wants to forget the hassle of traveling. Our product is a traveling website that allows you to receive information from trusted sources to assist with travel. Unlike Open Data, we provide a visual representation that is easy to use, utilizing a website that will be open source for the public to edit and use. Our product, Gemini, will assist travelers who are weary about traveling outside the country. As a company, our product aims to provide a open source alternative to other sources for travel.
